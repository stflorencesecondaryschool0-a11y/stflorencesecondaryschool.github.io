SPORTS AND EVENTS

At Florence, we believe that sports are a vital part of holistic education. Our well-rounded sports program helps learners build teamwork, discipline, fitness, and confidence. We provide opportunities for every student to participate, discover their talent, and excel both locally and nationally.

We offer a variety of sports, including:

  • Football (Soccer) – Our most celebrated sport, with strong school teams for both boys and girls.

  • Netball & Basketball – Encouraging agility, teamwork, and competitiveness.

  • Athletics & Cross-country – Building endurance, speed, and determination.

  • Volleyball & Handball – Promoting teamwork and quick reflexes.

  • Indoor Games – Chess, scrabble, and table tennis to sharpen the mind.
