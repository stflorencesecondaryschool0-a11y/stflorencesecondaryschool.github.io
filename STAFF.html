Our Staff

At St. Florence we are proud of our dedicated and passionate staff who form the backbone of our school community. Each member contributes uniquely to ensure that learners receive the best education, care, and guidance.

Teaching Staff

Our teachers are highly qualified professionals committed to learner-centered teaching. They not only deliver subject knowledge but also mentor students, nurture talents, and inspire excellence.

Head of Departments (HoDs): Coordinate teaching and ensure quality in their subject areas.

Class Teachers: Provide guidance, mentorship, and close monitoring of learners’ progress.

Subject Teachers: Specialists in various subjects, delivering engaging and effective lessons.

Administrative Staff

The administration team ensures smooth running of school operations and supports both teachers and learners.

Head Teacher: Provides overall leadership and vision.

Director of Studies: Oversees academics, curriculum, and performance.

Bursar / Accountant: Manages financial matters and school budgeting.

Secretary: Handles communication and school records.

Support Staff

Our non-teaching staff play an important role in creating a safe, clean, and supportive environment.

Librarians & ICT Technicians: Provide access to knowledge resources and technology support.

Matron & Health Officer: Ensure the health, safety, and welfare of students.

Cooks & Caterers: Provide nutritious meals for learners and staff.

Security Personnel & Groundskeepers: Keep the school secure and the environment neat.

Commitment
